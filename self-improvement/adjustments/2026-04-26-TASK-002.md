# TASK-002 Adjustment Log

## What changed
- Added `volume` and `open_interest` to parsed weather markets.
- Updated `choose_best_market()` to skip contracts with known total open interest below 1,000.
- Updated morning scan recommendations to return `MARKET_DISAGREES` when a market has volume above 5,000 and the model differs from the market ask by more than 10 cents.
- Added unit tests covering the low-open-interest skip and high-volume disagreement flag.

## Files changed
- `src/weatherlab/pipeline/_markets.py`
- `src/weatherlab/pipeline/morning_scan.py`
- `tests/test_morning_scan.py`

## Why
High-volume markets may already include informed pricing around station or airport bias, so the recommendation engine should avoid fighting liquid market consensus. Low-open-interest contracts are also too thin for reliable recommendations.

## Caveats
- The open-interest filter only skips markets when open interest is present and below 1,000. Markets without open-interest data remain eligible so older fixtures and incomplete API payloads do not get treated as zero-liquidity contracts.
