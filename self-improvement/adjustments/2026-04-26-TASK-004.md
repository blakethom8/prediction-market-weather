# TASK-004 Adjustment Log

## What Changed
- Wired intraday observed temperature checks into `_recommendation_for_city`.
- Added a same-day local-time gate so observed temps only affect live scans for the market date.
- Added a strong `BUY` path when an observed max reaches an `>=` contract threshold by 10 AM local.
- Added a one-step downgrade when observed max is more than 3F below forecast after 11 AM local.
- Added focused unit coverage for the observed-threshold buy and observed-lag downgrade rules.

## Files
- `src/weatherlab/pipeline/morning_scan.py`
- `tests/test_morning_scan.py`

## Why
Observed ASOS temperatures become more reliable than morning forecasts as the day progresses. If the day has already reached an above-threshold contract, the recommendation should treat that as stronger evidence. If observed temps are still materially below forecast by 11 AM, the scan should reduce confidence before recommending action.

## Caveats
- The strong observed-temperature buy only applies to `>=` threshold contracts. Bucket contracts can still lose if temperatures continue past the bucket, and `<=` contracts are not confirmed by an observed max.
- Intraday adjustments are limited to same-day scans using each station's local timezone to avoid applying live observation rules to historical or mocked scan dates.
