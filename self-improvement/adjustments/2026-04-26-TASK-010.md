## Why
The optimal East Coast betting window is 10-11am PDT, not 8am. This scan runs at 10:30am
(east window) and 12pm (central+west) to surface threshold plays where intraday observed
temps confirm the forecast signal.

## Caveats
- Only surfaces threshold contracts (>= and <=), never buckets
- Requires observed_max_so_far_f within 3°F of threshold to be actionable
- Crons still need to be added to OpenClaw or system scheduler
