# TASK-011 Adjustment Log

## What changed
- Added `is_threshold_contract(market)` to classify `>=` and `<=` contracts as thresholds and `between` contracts as buckets.
- Added `contract_type` to morning scan city output rows.
- Downgraded bucket BUY recommendations to WATCH unless they meet ColdMath-style criteria: at least a 10F forecast gap from the bucket edge and an equivalent market price of at least 85c.
- Added `[BUCKET - watch only]` notes to bucket recommendations in the scan report.
- Added tests proving a high-edge bucket is downgraded while a threshold contract with the same edge remains BUY.

## Files changed
- `src/weatherlab/pipeline/_markets.py`
- `src/weatherlab/pipeline/morning_scan.py`
- `tests/test_morning_scan.py`

## Why
Bucket contracts require narrow temperature accuracy, while threshold contracts can win across a range. This keeps ordinary edge-based bucket picks out of actionable BUY output unless they satisfy the stricter ColdMath setup.

## Caveats
- The ColdMath exception is interpreted as a bucket NO setup using the NO-equivalent price (`1 - yes_ask`) because the city recommendation path only stores the market ask.
- Existing unrelated worktree changes were left untouched.
